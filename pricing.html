Frequently Asked Questions

How does Predictive Protection pricing work?

Pricing is based on the size of the landscape under predictive protection, typically ranging between $2–12 per km² per month depending on landscape size and monitoring complexity.
What is included in Edge Infrastructure Leasing?

Our leasing model includes installation, monitoring, and maintenance of solar-powered autonomous edge AI nodes. Instead of selling hardware, we offer a full-service model at $250–$300 per node per month.
Who is the Biodiversity Intelligence Dashboard for?

Our investor-grade dashboard is designed for climate funds, corporations, and ESG investors. Subscribers gain access to live biodiversity metrics, vegetation health analytics, soil carbon monitoring, species trends, and ecosystem risk alerts.
What are Climate Security Credits™?

We partner with carbon project developers to enable premium carbon credits with reduced permanence risk. Traditional credits often trade between $10–20 per ton; security-backed credits command higher premiums. On Edge Safaris receives a platform and monitoring fee for protection services.
Can governments partner with On Edge Safaris?

Yes. We partner with governments to deploy predictive protection systems across large protected landscapes. Multi-year infrastructure contracts are customised per landscape depending on deployment scale, supporting national biodiversity protection and climate resilience.
How do I get started with On Edge Safaris?

Reach out through our contact form or email info@onedgesafarisAI.com. After an initial consultation, we’ll design a solution tailored to your landscape protection and biodiversity monitoring needs.
